Welcome to Smart Revision SMS System

A revolutionary SMS-based platform designed to help students revise their lessons anywhere in Rwanda, without the need for internet or smartphones.

How It Works

  • Students send an SMS with the subject and level, e.g., REVISE MATH S5.
  • The system replies with revision questions via SMS.
  • Students respond with answers through SMS.
  • The system scores the answers and sends feedback via SMS.

Objectives

  • Provide accessible revision content to all students, regardless of phone type.
  • Enable affordable and convenient self-assessment.
  • Support educators and parents in monitoring student progress.
